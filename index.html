About Us

August 7, 2021

NOLIA is a culinary blog based in Pontianak, West Kalimantan – Indonesia. This blog is owned and founded by Nopiana Sebah and Melia Dwi Lestari, 2 people who can be said to be friends for only 2 years who have a big passion in culinary. NOLIA is our gallery, we created this blog to capture our moment from food hunting. NOLIA stands for Nopi and Melia. At first, we were confused about what name to use. However, after discussing it, we decided to abbreviate our names, namely "NOpi" and "MElia".

Mekuru Ramen

Pontianak, August 14, 2021

This is the most delicious ramen in Pontianak we've ever tried. The owner and chef are real Japanese, so no wonder the ramen tastes authentic. We also don't need to go far to Japan, just in Pontianak. The chewy texture of the noodles, the thick and brothy, and the toppings provided further complement the ramen and make it a delicious. The price is affordable, starting from 30000 rupiah. This ramen is highly recommended and worth to try. If you have any great suggestion to go, do not hesitate to leave a comment on our blog, we would love to read it from you.

Snack Platter

Pontianak, January 10, 2022

This is a snack consisting of fried potatoes sprinkled with chili powder with a spicy sauce dipping and cold lychee tea. The delicious spicy taste of these fried potatoes is perfect to mix with cold lychee tea which refreshes the throat. It is perfect to enjoy when relaxing and when hanging out with friends in hot weather because of the hot sun during the day.

Personal Experience

Melia: According to personal experience, I have tried various foods, drinks, culinary in various places and restaurants. There are very good, not bad, and very bad food. Not only that, sometimes I find waiters who are less friendly to consumers. Usually, if I meet a waiter who is not friendly, I am lazy to go back to their restaurant. But, there are also waiters who are very friendly and make such a good impression on me that I want to come back to that restaurant again. I can't wait to get back to exploring the culinary.

Nopi: I've tried delicious food, namely at the Dare chicken stall which is located at Jl. HM Suwignyo, Sungai Jawi, Bangkong Pontianak, Pontianak City, West Kalimantan. Initially I was recommended by my friend, then I became very curious to try the food, so I went with several people to try eating at the Dare chicken stall. I tried to order grilled chicken. After I tried to eat the food I admit the food is delicious and satisfying. For friends who really like culinary, I highly recommend you to try the food at the Ayam Dare stall, because apart from the delicious food, the place is clean and the service is also friendly and the location is strategic beside the road. The only drawback is that the place is not spacious and it takes too long to wait for the food to be served.
